About Me

I started this journey in 2020 through Skillcrush where I learnt to design and code static and responsive websites. I have enjoyed the process of starting with an idea and then bringing it to life through colour and interaction. What interests me in web design is how much we rely on them for businesses to promote their services. Without a solid site, your business won't shine above the competition. I'm here to help you create your dream site from inception to completion. Recently relocated to the east coast USA, I love nothing more than exploring new places, hiking with my dogs, and drinking speciality coffee.


Looking forward to connecting and creating with you soon!
